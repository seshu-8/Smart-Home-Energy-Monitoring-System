IMPORTANT SAFETY NOTES
─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
1. The ACS712 and ZMPT101B interface with MAINS VOLTAGE (230V AC).
   - Do NOT touch IP+/IP- or AC terminals when power is on
   - Use a proper plastic enclosure for all mains connections
   - For learning/testing, use a low-voltage (5-12V DC) test bench first
   - The LOW-VOLTAGE side (OUT, VCC, GND) is safe for ESP32

2. ADC Pin restrictions on ESP32:
   - GPIO34, GPIO35 are INPUT-ONLY ADC1 pins — correct for sensors
   - GPIO32-GPIO39 are ADC1 — use these for analog sensors
   - DO NOT use GPIO0, GPIO2 (strapping pins) for sensors

3. Power supply:
   - USB 5V → ESP32 VIN works fine for bench testing
   - For permanent installation: use 5V/2A USB wall adapter
   - Never power ESP32 from the mains relay output directly
